My Thoughts on Progressive Enhancment

I think progressive enhancement is similar to introducing the 14th amendment to the web. Progressive enhancement allows all who are interested in developing the internet, a fair shot at doing so. Focusing on the content ensures that each person will get at a minimum what is most important to the webpage. I love the analogies Gustafson used for HTML, CSS, and JS. It helped me understand the concept of Progressive Enhancement and its interaction with each language.
